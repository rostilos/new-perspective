Perspective Studio team has been working on a fashion marketplace on the VnEcoms modules for a long time. The project covers all stages of development, from UI/UX design to QA and testing. Today it is already in pre-deployment condition. So we have a lot to talk about and are happy to share.

by Bogdan Gryvnak

What does the fashion marketplace based on Magento 2 look like?

Often, the Magento platform is associated with the development of online stores. Nevertheless it allows businesses to scale up to the multi vendor marketplace. The following components of the marketplace system are Merchant Administration System, Product Information Management, Order Management System, Billings, Reporting&Analytics.

During the development of fashion marketplace, we applied a group of modules from VnEcoms. However, the modules do not cover all customer needs, which is a normal practice. After all, every business has its peculiarities. The customization has been subject to many processes and we would like to tell more about them.

What has been processed and why?

  1. Firstly, we have simplified the process of seller registration. The following logic was laid, there is a form filled in by the seller on the page of the marketplace:

    The data from the form gets into the administrative panel. The main admin receives notifications to the e-mail address as well as on the admin panel about a new request and confirm it.

  2. Registration of the order was automatized. How it looks: the seller presses the eccept button when receiving the order and automatically creates an invoice, shipment, and the delivery of waybills. Also added an opportunity to edit the order.

  3. The management of orders was modified. Below you can see the view of the personal account before and after modification. Orders are distributed according to the status: new, processing, combined, cancelled. For a seller, it is way more convenient to track the order.

  4. The Vendor RMA from VnEcoms was modified to meet the needs of the client. Previously, his function allowed making a return from only one seller at a time. We have created a logic so that when returning, you can choose products of any seller. It became easier for the buyer to manage the process of registration and returning goods.

  5. Another case is the development of the delivery function. It's a separate story. Since it was international multi vendor marketplace, the owner has chosen DHL as the logistics operator for order delivery.

    In the Magento platform there is a module for the DHL delivery method, but not for the marketplace, VnEcoms also has it, however it is designed to work for one seller, so that is one DHL account. Both options did not solve the technical task. As a result, we have redesigned the module provided by VnEcoms to work with one account.

    During the process, there was a problem with the correctness of ratings (UK presence). As it turned out, at that time DHL API did not take into account the Brexit fee. After that, we finalized the functionality. Also, a function for reservation of the address ban of shipments was developed.

    Now it looks like this. The seller gets a new order and clicks accept, then automatically creates an invoice, shipment with the generation of packages and receiving shipping labels, and a tracking number from DHL. The order status changes to processing. Once the invoice is created, the DHL Address Ban button is available.

    Additionally, a feature has been developed that sends requests to the DHL API twice a day on cron and checks the post statuses. If it's delivered, then the order is considered final and only after that, the seller can make a return. The buyer can also monitor the status of the order through the tracking number in his personal account. This mechanism has been used to ensure that the tracking number is automatically displayed on the DHL page.

  6. In this case we had to realize a very difficult logic of pricing. We have developed a price-setting module specifically for the client's technical task. It was important not to break the basic function of Magento.

    The first thing that complicated the mechanism of pricing is the geography of marketplace. It has different stores depending on the buyer's region: UK, USA, EU, GLOBAL. Accordingly, for different buyers, there are different taxes, different costs of delivery and so on. In general, the cost is calculated taking into account six indicators. That is why the same goods in different stores can be fundamentally different in meaning of price.

    We have created the function in such a way that the seller in his office displays the cost of the goods, and the final cost takes into account all the indicators and is calculated automatically for each store individually. On the site, the buyer sees the final cost of the product, which is formed according to the logic of pricing.

    The seller has retained all basic marketing logic (special price, group price and tier price) at the level of the page, and the store owner can manage catalogue price.

  7. For transactions on the site used the payment service Stripe, it is customized accordingly to business needs. When creating or supporting regular online stores, it was not necessary to meet such a function as the division of payment between several participants. In this case, we met a task that says the whole payment does not fall on the account of the owner of the market, but only the commission.

    VnEcoms has a module for Stripe, but it did not cover our needs. Thanks to the modification, the module removes funds from the bank card and assigns the accounts to the owner of the business and the seller of goods. The owner of the market is given a сommission and the spent parts as well as the cost of delivery. The seller receives at the expense of the product sum, which was made at its creation, with the deduction of the basic commission.

Modules from other vendors

In addition to VnEcoms modules, more than a dozen other vendor modules were used to cover the customer's needs. Some of them we've installed without changes, and other are customised:

  1. Amasty_Magento 2 layered Navigation - for filtering products.

    Additionally, we have extended the functionality of the module as follows:

    • if you get on the category page, all filters are available
    • in the process of filtering, filters that were previously “disappeared” are now visible, although they are inactive.

  2. BSSCommerce_Magento Multiple Store View Pricing.

    A group of modules was used to install price points at the page level.

  3. FME_Magento 2 Product Image Zoom. Photo Gallery & Product Image Gallery.

    The modules are installed for the product image zoom and gallery in general.

  4. Age Array_Form Builder

    The module allowed the client to create forms conveniently and place them on the site pages.

  5. Mageplaza_Blog

    They used extensions to create a blog for sellers.

  6. Mageplaza_StoreSwitcher

    Enabled us to redirect our clients from different countries to the right side and switch to the right currency.

  7. Mageplaza_SocialLogin

    The module provides client login through social networks.

  8. Ebizmarts_MailChimp

    The module that manages mail.

  9. Modules from Mirasvit

    Magento 2 Improved Asynchronous Re-indexing Mirasvit, Magento 2 Full Page Cache Warmer Extension for warming the cache and re-indexing from the admin, Knowledge Base Extension for creating a seller and customer knowledge base.
