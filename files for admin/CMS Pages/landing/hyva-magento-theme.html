Magento Hyvä Theme: What It Offers

01. Can I use Hyvä with my Luma-based theme?

Hyvä is a standalone theme designed for Magento, offering a completely different frontend approach than Luma.

02. Will my current modules work with Hyvä?

Most modules should work with Hyvä, but it's recommended to consult the list of 140+ compatible extensions or get in touch with the Hyvä community for support.

03. How do I integrate my modules with Hyvä?

For seamless integration, you can refer to Hyvä's extensive documentation or seek assistance from Perspective's certified Magento developers.

04. How can Perspective help me with Hyvä?

Perspective's expert Magento developers can provide swift installation, support, and customization for the Hyvä theme, ensuring a seamless experience and helping you take full advantage of Hyvä's performance improvements.

05. How does Hyvä compare to the Breeze theme for Magento 2?

While both themes aim to optimize store performance, Hyvä focuses on simplicity and reduced code complexity, while Breeze emphasizes decreasing JS stack complexity and script evaluation time. Consult Perspective's expert developers to determine the best theme for your store.
