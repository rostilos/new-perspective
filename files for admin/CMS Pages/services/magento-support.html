Prioritizing Proactive
Monitoring and Agile Response

Operating independently of the main development team, our support team is available 24/7. This ensures they're prepared to address and resolve any arising issues promptly.

1. Fully Automated Monitoring

A significant part of successful incident management lies in the accurate setup of monitoring systems. Our seasoned support team has hands-on experience with various tools across numerous projects. These automated tools promptly notify relevant parties, including client-side personnel if required, of any potential issues such as server downtime or low disk space.

2. SLA Commitments

To ensure complete transparency and reliability, we provide a Service Level Agreement (SLA). This contractual commitment outlines specific timelines for incident response, temporary solutions, and final fixes, reinforcing our pledge to our clients.

3. Streamlined Incident Processing

Over time, we have streamlined step-by-step processes for all potential scenarios and situations, enabling us to respond efficiently when time is money.

4. Expert Hardware Tuning

Many incidents are related to server-side software or hardware settings. Our DevOps and SysOps departments, well-versed in Magento architecture, can fine-tune your hardware for peak performance.

5. Thorough Autotests

Our QA team, divided into manual testers and auto-test developers, ensures that all crucial customer functionalities are covered by automated tests. This allows for rapid validation, especially during incident resolution.

6. Zero-Downtime Deployment

Leveraging cutting-edge technologies, we're capable of deploying updates without causing any downtime - an approach known as "zero-downtime deployment." We strive to maintain a 99% uptime for our clients.

7. Robust Security Measures

As an official Adobe Solution Partner and a top Magento Core contributor, we stay ahead of potential threats by monitoring OWASP TOP10 vulnerabilities and the CVE register regularly. We ensure compliance with all necessary government regulations and technical rules, providing robust protection, particularly for PCI-compliant merchants.

8. Continuous Improvement Suggestions

Given our active management of 30-50 Magento projects, we provide regular suggestions for market-driven improvements that could benefit your business. Your role is simply to prioritize these improvements, and we'll help you stay on the cutting edge of the market.
