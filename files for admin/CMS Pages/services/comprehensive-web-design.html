To identify weaknesses affecting conversion rates, we conduct a UX audit of your interface, including a thorough analysis of Google Analytics data (and more).

Advantages:

  • Quick implementation of changes
  • Verification of results through rigorous testing
  • Minimal risks
  • Cost-effective budgeting

Upon identifying interface shortcomings, we prioritize based on their impact on conversion rates. We follow the principle of rapid changes:

1. We break all tasks into sprints, each designed to deliver maximum benefit to your business. The first sprint addresses the most critical interface errors that, when corrected, will significantly improve conversion rates.

2. We formulate hypotheses to rectify existing errors and test these using A/B testing.

3. In subsequent sprints, we address lower-priority issues and any additional requirements specified by the business owner.
